Travel

Places I've been and adventures I've had

🏔️ Montana

My Favorite Place in the World

Montana is the place I return to year-round with my family, no matter the season. Summer means lake days filled with sunshine and laughter, and winter brings the thrill of snowmobiling through snowy landscapes. There is nowhere else on earth that feels quite like home the way Montana does.

🌺 Hawaii

The Big Island, Maui & Honolulu

Hawaii is one of my all-time favorite vacation destinations. The humidity, the beaches, and the laid-back island energy make it impossible not to fall in love. I've been lucky enough to experience three islands — the Big Island, Maui, and Honolulu — and each one has its own unique magic.

🏛️ Greece

Blue Roofs & Ancient History

Greece was one of the most breathtaking trips I've ever been on. I was invited by my older cousin to celebrate her graduation, and we explored the main cities together. The iconic blue roofs, stunning coastlines, and rich ancient history made me fall completely in love with this country.

🎡 London

Study Abroad — Fall 2025

I had the incredible opportunity to live in London for four months through my BYU study abroad program focused on British Literature. Exploring a new city, immersing myself in its culture, and discovering hidden gems around every corner made London a place that will always have a special place in my heart.
